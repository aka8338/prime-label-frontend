Smarter Labels

  • Home
  • Smarter Labels

Beyond ink: your clinical label, reimagined.

Smart labels are more than digital replicas of printed stickers — they are living interfaces that evolve with your study, engage with patients, and connect seamlessly to your systems. Designed for the modern trial, clinicalLabel.io turns every label into a secure, dynamic, extensible asset.

Feature-Rich by Default

More than just text on a screen — our eLabels support text-to-speech, adjustable font sizes, image zoom, multilingual display, and embedded content such as instructional videos or links to IFUs.

Extensible Over Time

Need to add a new language, link a new video, or provide protocol-specific instructions mid-trial? You can. Our smart labels grow with your needs — without relabeling, reprinting, or relogging inventory.

Dynamic & Controlled

Update expiry dates. Adjust usage instructions. Modify cohort-specific details — all through validated workflows. Smart labels support real-time updates while protecting regulated fields through locked configurations.

Auditable by Design

Every scan. Every version. Every access event. Our system tracks usage patterns, timestamps, and even geolocation — providing powerful insight and a fully GxP-ready audit trail.

Seamless Integration

Use GS1, FHIR, or your own internal data model — our APIs and back-end architecture make it easy to integrate eLabels into your existing workflow and labeling ecosystem, from IRT to EDC to safety databases.

Role-Based Content Delivery

Whether scanned by a patient, nurse, or CRA — each user sees only what they need. Smart labels can deliver tailored content based on role, scan context, or study cohort.

Connected to Your Trial Ecosystem

Smart labels link directly to central study information — protocol updates, safety messages, recall alerts, training videos, or patient support services — keeping all stakeholders aligned and informed.

Supports Blinded Study Designs

Smart labels can limit or reveal content depending on study phase or user type — supporting double-blind, single-blind, or crossover designs without compromising integrity.

Clinical Trials will go digital. Join the journey with us

Your label doesn’t need to be static. With clinicalLabel.io, it becomes an active participant in your trial — reducing errors, increasing compliance, and elevating patient experience.
Let your label do more. Let it be smart.

In a digital world, your clinical label should be as smart as your science.
