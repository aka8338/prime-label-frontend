Driven by Innovation. Grounded in the Patient Experience

Clinical eLabels are the digital evolution of traditional medication labels — accessible through a simple QR code. They deliver clear, multilingual, real-time information directly to patients’ smartphones, going far beyond the limitations of printed labels. Whether it’s updated expiry dates, text-to-speech features, or links to educational content, eLabels ensure every patient can understand and trust their medication. If you’re responsible for patient safety, regulatory compliance, or trial efficiency — this matters. Discover how a smarter label transforms the clinical experience.
