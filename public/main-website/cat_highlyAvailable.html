High Availability

  • Home
  • High Availability

Access label information 24/7 from any location, no delays.

Clinical trials don’t stop at 5 p.m. — and neither should your label access. In a global study, participants may be taking their medication across multiple time zones, at all hours of the day or night, in urban hospitals or remote villages. When patients need label information, it must be there — instantly, reliably, every time.

clinicallabel.io was built with a commitment to high availability from the ground up. Our cloud infrastructure is architected for global scale, using multiple geographically redundant server locations, load balancing, failover systems, and real-time health monitoring to ensure that your labels are always accessible — no matter where, no matter when.

Our approach aligns with WCAG (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines) and considers principles outlined in Section 508 of the U.S. Rehabilitation Act, promoting equitable digital access for elderly users and people with disabilities.

Why It Matters

As the industry begins to shift toward live digital labels as the primary source of truth — with the physical label serving only as backup — high availability becomes mission-critical. ClinicalLabel.io is engineered to serve that future, starting now
