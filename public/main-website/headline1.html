One Label, Endless possibilities

Rethink What a Label Can Be In today's clinical trials, the physical label is reaching its limits. Packed with regulatory content, multilingual text, and protocol-specific details, it's constrained by physical dimensions, adhesive challenges, and the properties of the container it's applied to. With clinicallabel.io, we introduce the concept of One Label — a digital-first, patient-centered approach that replaces fragmentation with flexibility, and waste with intelligence.

Why Physical Labels Fall Short

Physical labels remain a regulatory requirement and will continue to play an important role in clinical trials for years to come. However, as studies become more complex and patient-centric, the limitations of physical labels become increasingly apparent. From space constraints to challenges with updates and global usability, traditional labeling can't always keep pace with the dynamic nature of modern trials. That’s where digital eLabels can complement and enhance — not replace — the labeling experience.

Phsyically Limited
Small vials, curved pens, and frozen surfaces leave little space for critical instructions. A digital label has infinite surface area.

Expensive & Fragile
Booklet labels are costly to print, verify, and distribute — and are prone to tearing, smudging, and weathering.

Static in a Dynamic World
Trials evolve. Physical labels don’t. Amendments, expiry changes, or added guidance require relabeling — digital labels just update.

Format-Dependent
Different dosage forms require custom labels. A syringe label is not the same as a bottle label. One digital label fits all.

One step loser to the future of clinical trials

With clinicallabel.io, you’re not just digitizing your label — you’re transforming it into a living, breathing part of your clinical trial. One label that works across countries, formats, languages, and study phases. A label that doesn’t get torn, smudged, or outdated. One Label. One Standard. One Step Closer to the Future of Trials.

Endless Content
From translations to usage videos and even text-to-speech — all from a single QR code.

Universal Accessibility
Elderly users can enlarge text. Visually impaired users can listen. Everyone gets the label in their language and format.

Real-Time Updates
Expiry extension? New instructions? Adjust the label digitally — no reprint, no return, no delay.

Pooled Supply Support
Use a single label to support multiple trials. With dynamic routing, a product knows which protocol it belongs to.

Patient-Specific Flexibility
One label can adapt content based on site, language, or cohort — all controlled securely through our backend.

Sustainable by Default
Reduce printing, rework, and waste. Better for your budget, better for the environment.

Audit-Ready by Design
Every version, view, and update is logged and traceable — effortlessly inspection-ready.

Improved Compliance
When patients can understand the label, they’re more likely to follow the protocol — and finish the study.
